Built a playable Othello game and AI engine from scratch in Java, featuring a minimax opponent with three levels of difficulty.

## Highlights
- Built a playable Othello game from scratch in Java, which highlighted available moves and automatically decided the winner
- Allowed players to toggle between playing a human player and a custom AI with three levels of difficulty
- Implemented a minimax program with variable-depth search to play against humans
- Iteratively played the computer against itself while tweaking heuristics to optimize performance
